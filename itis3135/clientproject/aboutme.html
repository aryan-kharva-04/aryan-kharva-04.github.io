About Vaishali Kharva

Welcome to Bollywood Threads! I'm Vaishali Kharva, the founder and creative mind behind this vibrant Indian fashion business.

Passion for Fashion

Since my childhood, I've been enamored by the rich and diverse tapestry of Indian fashion. From the intricate embroidery to the vibrant colors, every detail tells a story of tradition, culture, and elegance. My passion for fashion led me to pursue my dream of bringing the essence of Bollywood glamour to fashion enthusiasts worldwide.

Celebrating Diversity

At Bollywood Threads, we celebrate the diversity of Indian fashion, offering a wide range of styles that cater to every taste and occasion. From timeless classics to contemporary trends, each piece in our collection is crafted with precision and care, ensuring impeccable quality and style.

Our Commitment

Our commitment goes beyond providing exceptional products; it extends to fostering a community of fashion enthusiasts who share our love for Indian culture and craftsmanship. We strive to create an inclusive and welcoming space where customers can explore, express, and embrace their unique sense of style.

Join Us

Whether you're looking for the perfect outfit for a special occasion or simply want to indulge in the beauty of Indian fashion, Bollywood Threads invites you to join us on this enchanting journey. Let's celebrate style, tradition, and the timeless allure of Bollywood together.


Thank you for choosing Bollywood Threads!
