Bollywood Threads

Overview

  • Description and Purpose: The application will serve as an online platform for my client's small business that sells Indian womens clothing.

  • Intended Users: The intended users include potential customers for my client's business. These mostly include people living in the Research Triangle who are looking to buy Indian womenswear.

  • Content: The content will include a gallery of the collection that's for sale currently, a way to book appointments to come purchase pieces, and an information page about the owner and contact information.

Dynamic Functionality

  • Home Page Photo Slideshow and Interactive Images - I will use Javascript to implement a photo slideshow on the home page. Images will show key images of clothes that are on sale and of my client. Images of upcoming exhibitions in this slideshow will redirect the user to the 'Upcoming Events and Exhibitions' page of the website.

    Example: Amazon uses a slideshow on their main page where if you click on one of the images, it'll bring you to a different part of the website.

  • Book An Appointment Form - This form will implement Javascript by validating the user email address and phone number.

    Example: Apple uses a form on their website when signing in, which validates the user's information before logging in.
